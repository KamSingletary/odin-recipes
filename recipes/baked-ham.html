Sugar Glased Ham

Baking a ham is not hard, you just need to watch the temperature to make sure it heats through without over cooking. Adding a brown sugar glaze to baked ham takes it to the next level without being overly sweet.

For this recipe a bone-in fully cooked ham cooks for about 12-14 minutes per pound (a 9lb ham will take about 2 1/4 hours).

Ingredients

  • Bone in ham
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Combine butter, salt, pepper, and brown sugar. Brush over ham.
  3. Remove plastic disk on bone. Place ham flat side down in a roast pan and cover with tin foil. Roast 12-15 minutes per pound.
  4. 15 minutes before ham is done remove from oven.
  5. Brush with remaining glaze and place in over for 15 minutes.
  6. Let rest for 10 mintures before cutting.
